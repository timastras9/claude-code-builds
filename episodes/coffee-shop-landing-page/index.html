About Us

Bean & Brew started as a roasting drum in a garage and a folding table at the Saturday market. Fourteen years later we're still small on purpose: one roaster, one espresso machine, and beans sourced directly from farms we've visited in Colombia and Ethiopia.

Come for the coffee, stay for the window seat. Open daily, 7am to 4pm.
